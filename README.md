# Welcome to the official Telum Solaris GitHub!

Telum Solaris is a minecraft 1.21.1 mod aiming to add new weapons, armor and enemies in ways common to MMORPGs.  
This is my first ever mod, and I'm likely to continue working on it.  
My ultimate goal is to have a huge amount of weapons and enemy mechanics to make the game have a better progression and be more interesting!  

---
# Currently, we have:
## New weapon types:
This mod adds dagger, longsword/greatsword, halberd, katana and scythe variants to diamond and iron items!

### There is also a new tool material: 
Steel!

Obtained by smelting iron in a furnace. This item also has the variants mentioned above, alongside a shield and block form.

### I got a gemstone chunk, now what?
After getting your first gemstone with a netherite pickaxe, you can smelt it in a blast furnace.  
After collecting your refined gemstone, try putting it in a stonecutter.  
Here you can make the perfect version - which can be used to craft armor and weapons - and the tokens.  
These tokens are the repair material for your new-found tools!
